What I care about applications I use?

There’s been an abundance of applications implemented nowadays for all kinds of use-cases.

In the following order of importance: - Privacy - Systems Performance - Fault Tolerance - Programmatic Usability - Anonymity

Most software written nowadays do not focus on the above metrics. Instead they focus on GUI-friendliness and ease of use as the primary focus, which makes program composition difficult going-forward.
